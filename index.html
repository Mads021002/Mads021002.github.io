About Me

My name is Mads Damgaard, I'm currently 21 years old living in Copenhagen, Denmark. I recently decided that I want to pursue a bachelors degree in Software Development at ITU, starting from september 2024. Until then I will be spending April and May studying math from 8:30 to 11:30 at Niels Brock.

When I was 19, I moved to Barcelona, Spain. Post my high school graduation (HHX), I took opportunity to take an internship abroad (PIU), where I got a two year education as a commercial assistant with sales, within one year instead.
Barcelona thought me a ton of stuff about myself, culture and how to navigate uncertain situations. Being in a foreign country, where you don't speak the language, while working for a hectic pre-product market fit startup builds gut!

I have a ton of interesting stories, if you want to hear more, feel free to fill out the contact form below or give me a shout on LinkedIn.
